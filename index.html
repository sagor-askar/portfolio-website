Know About Akik Fashions Ltd.

Akik Fashions is an agent / buying house specialized in garments, textiles and accessories. Established in 2010 to create more transparency and reliability and with the confidence that, once reliability can be provided regarding the required price and quality standards, there are ample business opportunities in Bangladesh, world second largest garment exporter. We are confident of accomplishing our vision and can work with a wide range of RMG industries through our diverse array of services.

Sustainability

Akik Fashions recognize that modern slavery is global issue and in apparel industry it is overlooked. AF is committed to ensure that there is no modern slavery or human trafficking in our partner factories or even not acceptable in any part of our business. This Policy also covers the issue of modern slavery, which is comprehensively define the current burning issue in apparel and textile industry. Akik Fashions strongly prohibit any type of forced labour. If we find any case related to modern slavery and verify a case of forced labour, we take immediate action. The consequence is immediate termination of business relationships and there will be no chance for corrective actions.
